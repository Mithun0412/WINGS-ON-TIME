Project Report: Wings On Time

This project presents a dashboard-based solution to monitor airline delays and their impact on passengers. It addresses key operational concerns such as average delay time, best and worst performing airlines, delay trends, and data-driven decisions to improve service and operations.

This project was initiated to explore real-world airline operations and improve schedule adherence using data analytics. With increasing flight delays affecting passenger satisfaction, this dashboard helps stakeholders take action through insights and trends.

Objectives include:

  • Monitor airline on-time performance
  • Identify causes and patterns of delays
  • Support strategic decision-making

The dataset includes flight details such as airline names, origin and destination airports, delay types and duration, satisfaction scores, and more.

  • Source: Simulated operational dataset
  • Fields: Airline, Departure/Arrival Delay, Reason, Ticket Class, Satisfaction Score
  • Coverage: Over 10,000 flights across multiple airports and airlines

  • Missing or invalid values were handled using imputation techniques.
  • New fields like delay duration category and satisfaction class were derived.
  • IBM Cognos Data Modules and Excel were used for data shaping.

The dashboard is tailored for airline managers, airport staff, and analysts.

  • Goals: Clarity, interactivity, responsiveness
  • Layout: Navigation with tabbed structure (Home, Dashboard, Analytics, Report)
  • Visuals: Line chart, heatmap, bubble chart, box plot, tree map

  • Bubble Chart: Delay reasons vs duration
  • Box Plot: Delay variation per airline
  • Heatmap: Delay by time and airport
  • TreeMap: Delay proportion by reason
  • Filters: Airline, time, delay type
  • KPIs: Avg Delay, On-time %, Top Airlines

  • Airlines A and B have superior on-time rates
  • Most delays cluster around 2–5 PM, especially at hubs
  • Technical and late aircraft issues are leading causes
  • Passenger satisfaction drops sharply with delays over 30 min
